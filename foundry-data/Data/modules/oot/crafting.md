# OOT Crafting System Summary

## Overview

Crafting magical items requires **two checks**:
1. **Creation Check** - For physically making the item
2. **Enchanting Check** - For imbuing the item with magical properties

The skill used for each check varies based on the item and is **manually selectable**.

## Enchanting DC by Rarity

| Rarity     | DC  |
|------------|-----|
| Uncommon   | 15  |
| Rare       | 18  |
| Very Rare  | 21  |
| Legendary  | 25  |
| Artifact   | 30  |

---

## Crafting Check Results (Quirks)

Based on the difference between the crafting check result and the DC:

| Result - DC | Outcome                      |
|-------------|------------------------------|
| -13 or less | Total failure, item destroyed |
| -12 to -9   | Three flaws                  |
| -8 to -5    | Two flaws                    |
| -4 to -1    | One flaw                     |
| 0 to 4      | Nothing                      |
| 5 to 8      | One boon                     |
| 9 to 12     | Two boons                    |
| 13+         | Three boons                  |

## Manufacturing Flaws (d20)

| d20   | Flaw Name                        | Description | Item Types |
|-------|----------------------------------|-------------|------------|
| 1-6   | **Poor/Shoddy/Abysmal Handiwork** | Item is not well made. Weapons: **-1 penalty** to attack and damage rolls. Armour: **-1 penalty** to base AC. Other items: gains the Fragile quirk. | Armour, weapon |
| 7-8   | **Fragile** | Prone to breaking. Weapons: breaks on a **1** on d20 attack roll. Other items: roll **d20** on critical hit received; breaks on **1**. | All |
| 9-10  | **Unwieldy** | Poorly balanced. On a **1** on d20 for attacks, Dex saves, or Acrobatics checks: weapons fly **10 feet** randomly, other items cause you to fall **prone**. | All |
| 11-12 | **Degradable** | Degrades in water/corrosive environments. Roll **d20** after submersion or each hour in corrosive environment; breaks on **1**. | All |
| 13-14 | **Noisy** | Squeaks or rustles. **Disadvantage** on Dexterity (Stealth) checks while wearing/carrying, even if stowed. | All |
| 15    | **Pungent** | Distinctly off-putting odour. **Disadvantage** on Charisma checks vs creatures that dislike bad smells. Creatures have **advantage** on Wisdom (Perception) to detect you by smell. | All |
| 16    | **Heavy** | Weighs **twice as much** as normal. | All |
| 17    | **Garish** | Designs and colours are all wrong. **Disadvantage** on Charisma (Intimidation) checks vs creatures that can see the item. | All |
| 18    | **Mediocre Finish** | Looks like crap. Worth **half** its normal value. | All |
| 19    | **Under Insulated** | Conducts heat or is under-insulated. Take an additional **1d8 damage** of the same type when receiving cold or fire damage. | All |
| 20    | **Dangerous** | Critical fail range increases by 1 (e.g., 1 becomes 1-2 on d20) when attacking with, wearing, or holding this item. | All |

## Manufacturing Boons (d20)

| d20   | Boon Name                    | Description | Item Types |
|-------|------------------------------|-------------|------------|
| 1-2   | **Durable** | Hit points of this item are **tripled**. | All |
| 3-4   | **Unreactive** | Resists corrosion and rot. Roll **d20** when environmental effect would damage item; unaffected on **11+**. | All |
| 5-6   | **Lightweight** | Weighs **half as much** at no detriment. Heavy weapons lose the heavy property; non-heavy items gain the light property. | All |
| 7-8   | **Magnificent Finish** | Finish is on point. Worth **twice** its normal value. | All |
| 9-10  | **Flashy** | Looks really cool. **Advantage** on Charisma (Persuasion) checks vs creatures that can see the item. | All |
| 11-12 | **Insulated** | **Advantage** on Constitution saves vs cold weather effects. **Advantage** on saves vs *heat metal* spell when holding weapon/held item. | Armour, clothing, held item, weapon |
| 13-14 | **Grippy** | **Advantage** on ability checks and saves to resist being disarmed or having item taken against your will. | Held item, weapon |
| 15-16 | **Quick Release** | Quick to equip/stow. Don/doff time is **10x quicker** (bonus action or action for shields). | Armour, shield |
| 17-18 | **Aerodynamic** | Normal and long ranges increased by **50%** (rounded down). For ammunition, increases the firing weapon's range by 50%. | Ammunition, thrown weapon |
| 19    | **Perfect Balance** | Reroll the d20 on a **1** for: weapons (attack rolls), armour/clothing (Dex saves/Acrobatics). Must use new result. | Armour, clothing, weapon |
| 20    | **Artisanal Craftsmanship** | Weapons: **+1 bonus** to damage rolls. Armour: bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from nonmagical attacks reduced by **1**. | Armour, weapon |

---

## Notes

- Flaws and boons with restricted item types should be rerolled if they don't apply to the crafted item
- The essence used during enchanting determines the maximum number of enchanting boons possible
- Crafting and enchanting checks are separate rolls with separate quirk outcomes
