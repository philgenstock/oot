## Backstory
Elira learned her trade from her mother, a military armorsmith who supplied light cavalry. When Elira was sixteen, a vampire raid destroyed her town's garrison. The soldiers died because their armor failed - straps broke, leather rotted from necrotic damage, equipment fell apart.

Her mother died trying to repair a captain's armor mid-battle. Elira survived by hiding in the tanning workshop, surrounded by the pungent chemicals vampires avoided.

She spent the next decade researching how to create armor that could withstand vampire attacks - resistance to necrotic damage, reinforced stress points, blessed materials. When she heard about the Divina Virtus mission, she saw it as a chance to protect people the way she couldn't protect her mother.

## Crafting Philosophy
"Armor is a promise. I promise you this: if you wear my work and maintain it properly, it will save your life. I've kept that promise for thirteen years, through forty-three vampire encounters. Don't plan to break it now."
