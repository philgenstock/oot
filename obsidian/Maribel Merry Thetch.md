# Maribel "Merry" Thetch
**Race:** Halfling
**Role:** Cleric of Lathander (God of Dawn and Renewal)
**Age:** 67
**Height:** 3'4"

## Appearance
A grandmother figure with silver-streaked auburn hair kept in a practical braid, warm hazel eyes that crinkle at the corners from constant smiling, and hands marked with the calluses of hard work. Wears simple robes in sunrise orange and gold, with holy symbols woven throughout. Despite her age, moves with surprising energy and grace.

## Personality Traits
- **Relentlessly Optimistic:** Sees every setback as a lesson, every dawn as a new chance
- **Motherly:** Fusses over the crew's health, emotional state, and whether they're eating properly
- **Stern When Needed:** Can deliver a scolding that makes grown warriors feel like scolded children
- **Gallows Humor:** Uses cheerful sayings in dark situations ("Well, at least the vampire's having a worse day than us now!")
- **Faithful but Practical:** Believes the gods help those who prepare properly
- **Memory Keeper:** Knows everyone's birthday, favorite food, and personal struggles

## Divine Role
**Class:** Life Domain Cleric (Level 9)
**Deity:** Lathander, the Morninglord

### Divine Powers
- Powerful healing magic
- Turn Undead (particularly effective against vampires)
- Cleansing rituals for those suffering from vampire corruption
- Blessings and protective wards
- Divination for guidance
- Restoration of ability damage and curses

## Services Provided

### Cleansing of Madness & Corruption
- **Remove Curse:** Breaks vampire charm effects and domination
- **Lesser Restoration:** Heals ability damage from vampire feeding
- **Greater Restoration:** Reverses vampire charm, reduces exhaustion from terror
- **Calm Emotions:** Helps crew dealing with trauma and fear
- **Protection from Evil and Good:** Wards against vampire domination
- **Purification Rituals:** Multi-day ceremonies to cleanse vampire corruption before turning occurs

### Spell Scrolls
Creates spell scrolls for adventurers (prices vary by spell level):
- **Healing Scrolls:** Cure Wounds, Lesser Restoration, Prayer of Healing
- **Protection Scrolls:** Aid, Beacon of Hope, Death Ward, Protection from Evil
- **Offensive Scrolls:** Sacred Flame, Spiritual Weapon, Spirit Guardians
- **Utility Scrolls:** Augury, Divination, Locate Object
- **Anti-Vampire Scrolls:** Daylight, Dawn, Sunbeam (rare and expensive)

### Daily Blessings
Each morning at sunrise, performs a ship-wide blessing ceremony that provides minor benefits to the crew for the day.

## Notable Gear & Items
- **Holy Symbol of Lathander:** A golden sunburst pendant that can cast Daylight three times per day. Glows warmly in the presence of undead
- **Mace of Dawn:** A silvered mace that deals radiant damage to undead. The head is shaped like a rising sun
- **Healer's Satchel of Holding:** Contains extensive medical supplies, holy water, and healing herbs
- **Scroll Case of Preservation:** Protects spell scrolls from moisture and damage
- **Prayer Beads:** Each bead can be used to enhance a spell once per day
- **Blessed Bandages:** Cloth strips blessed during morning prayers, aid natural healing
- **Censer of Cleansing:** Burns incense that purifies an area of undead taint
- **Journal of Testimonies:** Records the stories of those she's helped, finds strength in reading them during dark times
- **Sunrise Lantern:** Never needs oil, provides warm light that's particularly comforting to those suffering from fear effects

## Backstory
Maribel was a village healer and midwife before taking holy orders late in life. After vampires attacked a neighboring village, she helped care for survivors and witnessed the psychological toll of vampire attacks - the fear, the nightmares, the lasting corruption.

She prayed for guidance and received a vision from Lathander showing her the Divina Virtus sailing toward a blood-red shore. Taking it as a divine calling, she tracked down the expedition and volunteered her services. She's been with the mission from the beginning, serving as both spiritual guide and trauma counselor to the crew.

## Clerical Philosophy
"Darkness flees before the dawn, dearies. No matter how long the night, no matter how deep the shadow, the sun always rises. We are the bringers of that dawn, the promise that evil's reign is temporary, and hope eternal."

## Special Services

### Trauma Counseling
Beyond magical healing, Merry provides emotional support:
- Private counseling sessions
- Group prayer and sharing circles
- Teaches coping methods for dealing with fear and loss
- Helps crew process difficult missions

### Corruption Tracking
Maintains careful records of crew exposure to vampire influence:
- Tracks bite victims for signs of turning
- Monitors psychological effects of vampire encounters
- Identifies crew members who need additional cleansing
- Can detect early signs of vampire corruption

### Funeral Rites
When crew members fall, she ensures proper rites are performed to prevent them from rising as undead. Keeps a memorial book with names and stories of the fallen.

## Scroll Crafting System
Merry can create scrolls during downtime:
- **Common Scrolls:** 1 day, moderate cost
- **Uncommon Scrolls:** 3 days, higher cost
- **Rare Scrolls:** 1 week, significant cost
- Materials can be provided by crew to reduce costs

She's willing to create custom scrolls for specific situations if given enough notice.

## Relationships
- **Maternal Concern** for Syla, worries about her nightmares
- **Prayer Partner** with Thalarion, though she wishes he'd let go of his grief
- **Blesses** Tormak's weapons and Elira's armor regularly
- **Herbal Exchanges** with Bramblethorn, they discuss the intersection of divine and natural healing
- **Gentle Nagging** of Garrick to be more careful with explosives
- **Strategy Sessions** with Kett about minimizing crew danger

## Quest Hooks
- Seeking holy relics from churches in vampire-controlled territories
- Wants to establish safe houses and shrines along the coast
- Interested in any survivors who need rescue or spiritual aid
- Will request escorts to perform cleansing rituals at sites of vampire atrocities

## Personal Mission
Merry believes that every person saved from vampires is a victory for the light. She keeps a string of beads, adding one for every life saved or cleansed. She's up to 237 beads and prays over each one every night.

## Daily Routine
- Pre-dawn meditation and prayer
- Sunrise blessing ceremony for the crew
- Morning healing rounds
- Afternoon scroll crafting or counseling sessions
- Evening vespers
- Night rounds checking on crew welfare

## Combat Role
While she prefers healing and support, Merry is surprisingly effective in combat against undead:
- Turns vampires and their spawn with powerful divine energy
- Provides healing and buffs to frontline fighters
- Uses radiant damage spells when needed
- Always positions herself to protect the most vulnerable

## Personal Quirk
Despite being a cleric of the god of dawn, she's a terrible morning person until she's had her tea. Crew members know not to approach with serious matters until after the sunrise ceremony.
