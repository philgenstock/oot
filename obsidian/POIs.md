# Points of Interest

## The Fanatics

### Purification Temple
Ruined shrines where they perform cleansing rituals.
Info dump about the fanatics

### The Schism Archive
Hidden records of the original break with the vampires. What heresy caused the split?


### The Inquisitor Camp
Mobile war parties hunting "the unclean." 
Should be avoided at the start, later raided and maybe purged

### The First Martyr's Tomb
Their holiest site, heavily guarded. Contains the truth about their founding.

---

## The Architect

### Abandoned Laboratory
Early experiments, failed creations still lurking.

### Flesh Gardens
Where raw materials are "grown." Disturbing but valuable alchemical components.

### The Showcase
A twisted museum of "perfect" creations. The Architect's ego on display.

### The Architect's Current Workshop
Moving camp of the architect. To be avoided earlier and raided later

---

## Nature (The Cursed Land)

### The Rot Heart Groves
Sentient tree clusters, some might be reasoned with.

### Druid Circle Ruins
What corrupted them? Residual power could fuel the city.

### The First Blight Site
Where the curse originated. High-level revelation location.

---

## The Scourge

### Bone Tides
Beaches where undead wash ashore endlessly. Why here?

### The Hive Crypts
Underground networks where undead mass. Dangerous shortcuts.

### Remnant Settlements
Former towns, now undead-infested. Loot and lore.

### The Hungering One
A massive undead entity that controls local scourge. Mini-boss.
